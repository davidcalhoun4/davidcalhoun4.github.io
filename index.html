Software Developer

What I love most about development is the use of a language to make something come alive on screen. As a dance choreographer, creating applications reminds me of using my movement vocabulary to make a dance piece.

My desire to dive into a career in development let me to attend Actualize's Coding Bootcamp. What a eye opening experience it was, affirming that development satisfied me as a creative and problem solver.
