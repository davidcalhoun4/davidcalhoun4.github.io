Software Development

What I love most about development is the use of a technology to make something come alive on screen. Simple concept but how fascinating. In 2020, I was interested in learning a new skill so I began to teach myself programming. As a dance choreographer creating applications reminds me of using my movement vocabulary to make a dance work. I quickly became consumed with the desire to deepen my programming skill set. That desire led me to attend Actualize's Coding Bootcamp. What a beautiful experience it was, affirming that development satisfied me as a creative and problem solver. My intention with being a web developer is to aid in making applications accessible to all.
